YomiWiki

Editorial Policy

YomiWiki is built as an edited archive, not a place for unchecked rumors to spread. Each article should add context, organization, and interpretation instead of simply repeating a claim.

Article Structure

  • Claim or record summary: what the page is documenting.
  • Context: where the record appears to come from and why it matters.
  • Reliability note: what is known, unknown, or disputed.
  • Editor interpretation: what pattern or cultural meaning the record may show.

Corrections

When a record is unclear, corrected, or contradicted by better information, the page should be updated. Readers can send corrections through the contact page.

Safety Rules

We remove personal data, private contact details, targeted harassment, adult material, hateful claims, and instructions that could help someone cause harm.
