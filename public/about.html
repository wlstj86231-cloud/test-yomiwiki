About YomiWiki

YomiWiki is an edited public archive for unusual records: occult references, internet folklore, regional rumors, strange experiences, and the way these stories move through communities.

The site does not treat every claim as fact. Articles are organized so readers can separate the original claim, known context, reliability notes, and editorial interpretation.

What We Publish

  • Publicly discussable folklore, rumors, and unusual experiences.
  • Contextual notes about how a story changed across time or language.
  • Editor observations that explain why a record is notable.

What We Avoid

  • Personal data, doxxing, harassment, hate, adult content, and unsafe instructions.
  • Unverified accusations against private individuals.
  • Copied posts without added context, commentary, or archival value.
