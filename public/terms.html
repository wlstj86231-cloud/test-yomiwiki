Terms of Use

By using YomiWiki, readers and contributors agree to use the site for lawful, respectful, and informational purposes.

Contributor Rules

  • Do not submit personal data, harassment, hate, adult content, or unsafe instructions.
  • Do not impersonate others or submit material you do not have the right to share.
  • Do not use the archive to target private individuals or spread unverified accusations.

Editorial Control

YomiWiki may edit, reject, hide, or remove content that violates these terms or reduces the reliability of the archive.

Account Access

Public reading does not require an account. Editing and discussion features may be limited while the archive is being reviewed for quality, safety, and spam prevention.
