Privacy Policy

YomiWiki collects only the information needed to operate the archive, protect the service, and understand site performance.

Information We May Process

  • Account username and authentication data when a user registers or logs in.
  • Article edits, revision history, comments, and timestamps.
  • Basic technical logs and analytics data such as page views, browser type, and approximate region.

Cookies and Analytics

The site may use analytics tools to measure traffic and improve navigation. Browser settings can be used to limit or block cookies.

User Generated Content

Comments, edits, and submissions may be stored with timestamps and account identifiers so the archive can prevent spam, investigate abuse, and apply corrections.

Contact
